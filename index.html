Core Technology Stack

Quantum Sensing & Metrology Pipelines

Click on each core framework component to explore its deep-tech architecture and processing methodology.

01
NV Magnetometry
Nitrogen-Vacancy diamond defects capturing sub-picotesla neural magnetic fields.

Our non-invasive active sensor array utilizes dry, room-temperature diamond cells embedded with micro-engineered Nitrogen-Vacancy defects. These spin-active defect centers record magnetic field variations directly through bone tissue without attenuation.

  • NV Defect Centers: Single-crystal diamond matrices.
  • Wearable Array: Distributed sensor layout mapping cortical structures.
  • Room-Temperature: Eliminates cryogenic helium shielding requirements.
02
Laser & ODMR Readout
Optical Detection of Magnetic Resonance using 532nm laser excitation.

Optical Detection of Magnetic Resonance (ODMR) monitors spin states within the diamond crystal lattice. A green initialization laser (532nm) and micro-coils polarize and sweep the defect spins, mapping neural magnetic fields onto fluorescent light intensity dips.

  • 532nm Laser Polarization: Optical spin-state initialization.
  • Fluorescence Dips: Precise resonance shift tracking.
  • Zeeman Splitting: Directly proportional to neural magnetic vectors.
03
Geomagnetic Cancellation
Real-time subtractive differential sensing removing ambient field noise.

Because the Earth's magnetic field and urban power lines are orders of magnitude stronger than neural activity, we employ active differential gradiometers. By subtracting reference field data, the device cancels background noise in real-time.

  • Active Gradiometry: Sub-nanotesla reference tracking.
  • 120 dB Noise Cancellation: Isolates signal in open environments.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Adapts to moving magnetic anomalies.
04
Spin Signal Processing
Optoelectronic arrays converting red photoluminescence into magnetic data.

High-sensitivity photodiode matrices capture spin-dependent red photoluminescence (637nm) emitted by the diamonds. The analog signals are digitized using low-noise, high-resolution converters to form spatial magnetic maps.

  • 637nm Red Photoluminescence: Spin-state fluorescent output.
  • Photodetector Grids: High quantum efficiency conversion.
  • Ultra-low Noise Amplifiers: Prevents readout amplification errors.
05
EEG/EOG Hybrid Calibration
Complementary classic biosensors optimizing spatial sensor tracking.

To account for micro-movements of the headset, classical dry EEG electrodes and EOG sensors are integrated. These sensors continuously monitor skin impedance and eye dipoles, providing real-time drift calibration to the quantum sensing pipeline.

  • Skin Impedance Monitoring: High-precision contact calibration.
  • Blink Saccade Reference: EOG-driven noise subtraction.
  • Hybrid Data Fusion: Merges electrical and magnetic signal grids.
06
Quantum-Inspired AI
Tensor network models resolving 3D magnetic source localization.

To translate scalp magnetic field coordinates into local neural currents, our custom Tensor Network algorithms solve the magnetic inverse problem. By modeling brainwaves as high-dimensional tensor contractions, our AI maps signals in 3D.

  • Tensor Network Contracting: Decodes multi-sensor correlations.
  • Inverse Problem Solvers: Localizes source currents down to 50 micrometers.
  • Low Latency Inferences: Executed instantly on dedicated edge silicon.
07
Real-Time Edge Processor
Low-power embedded systems scheduling deterministic sensor readouts.

A custom ARM-based micro-controller handles green laser driving, photodiode reading, and ODMR sweeps on a fraction-milliwatt power budget. The RTOS scheduler guarantees sub-10ms control signal execution.

  • 0.8W Active Budget: Long-term thermal safety and battery life.
  • Deterministic Scheduler: Avoids signal drops during cognitive tasks.
  • Secure Enclave: Encrypts raw quantum data locally before streaming.
08
Diamond Nanofabrication
Synthetic diamond growth with optimized Nitrogen density profiles.

We grow custom chemical vapor deposition (CVD) diamonds. By precisely doping nitrogen gas and controlling vacancies during crystal growth, we maximize spin density while preserving long coherence times.

  • Precision Doping: High concentration of active spin sensors.
  • Coherence Optimization: Prolongs spin-state sensitivity.
  • Laser Cleaving: Micro-structured dimensions for wearable arrays.
